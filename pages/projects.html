Leveraged tools and concepts like Python, PowerBI, Regression analysis, Anova and Machine Learning. Employed normalization techniques such as Z-scoring to standardize student scores across years and their peers.
  • Built a descriptive model to describe a student's academic achievements (e.g : Grades, GPA, Graduation,) based on Panorama survey questionairres (A survey to assess the student's thought process, via questions like: Do you feel hardwork is crucial to success?, Do you feel that liking the subject is crucial to academic success?, etc.)
