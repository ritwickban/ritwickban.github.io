About Me

Hey there! Do you know (Catchy line).

Hey there! I'm Ritwick, a Graduate Research Assistant at the University of Minnesota, where I dive deep into data analyses to help a local school district reduce ethnic and economic disparities. By night, I'm a superhero researcher for the Minnesota NLP group, working on a repository to unify major language models and a framework to evaluate them. Oh, and did I mention my gig as a data analyst for the Mobile Health Initiative? Yep, I'm tackling emigrant health issues with data too! - Nah not a big deal!

When I'm not swimming in data, I'm whipping up culinary masterpieces that would make Gordon Ramsay proud, playing soccer, badminton, and squash (yes, I have too much energy), hiking, ice-skating, or chasing the elusive northern lights. I'm also a music explorer and podcast aficionado, always on the lookout for the next great self-development episode.

Here's a quirky tidbit: I have strikingly amazing conversations and a delightfully off-beat perspective on life. I live for a healthy debate and will probably disagree with you 90% of the time—not because I'm difficult, but because it's fun to keep things spicy!

Want to know that I am for real? Let's grab a coffee at the nearest Caribou Coffee (Yes, I am turning into a true Minnesotan!) Who knows, we might just teach ChatGPT to appreciate a Knock-knock joke.
